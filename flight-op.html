COMPLEX SYSTEM DESIGN

Flight operations involve hundreds of moving parts—from managing 30+ aircraft fleets and tracking crew readiness, to assigning daily flight operations across multiple international routes. We designed a central control tower that distills immense data into actionable insights.

KEY PRODUCT PILLARS

  • Aircraft & Fleet Tracking: Centralized database combining registrations, hardware serial numbers, and utilization analytics per airframe.
  • Flight Scheduling: Streamlined views to handle upcoming flight numbers, assign available crew, and mitigate scheduling conflicts instantly.
  • Crew Management: Oversight of personnel resources from Captains to Cabin Crew, ensuring proper utilization hours and readiness status.

OPERATIONAL READINESS

A critical safety and business module. The dashboard evaluates specific flight plans (e.g., Passenger Charter, Medical Evacuation) based on the assigned route and aircraft capabilities, formulating real-time 'Total Risk Scores'. This allows operators to directly assess bid viability and safety metrics before a contract is signed.
